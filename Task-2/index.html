Welcome to the Trubit Page for Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma, fondly known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, is a name synonymous with elegance, power, and records. Born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, Rohit's journey in cricket has been nothing short of spectacular.

Cricketing Achievements

International Cricket

International Cricket Rohit made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2007, and since then, he has become one of the leading run-scorers and most successful captains. His aggressive yet graceful batting style has earned him admiration from fans and peers alike. Notably, Rohit is the only player to have scored three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs), a testament to his exceptional batting prowess. In 2019, Rohit was appointed as the vice-captain of the Indian cricket team in ODIs and T20Is, and he has seamlessly led the team to numerous victories.

Indian Premier League (IPL)

Rohit Sharma is an iconic figure in the Indian Premier League (IPL), having captained the Mumbai Indians since 2013. Under his leadership, the team has secured multiple IPL titles, solidifying his reputation as one of the most successful captains in the league's history.

Records and Milestones

First Player to Score Three Double Centuries in ODIs: Rohit Sharma achieved this remarkable feat against Australia, Sri Lanka, and West Indies.
Fastest T20I Century: In 2017, Rohit Sharma scored a breathtaking century off just 35 balls against Sri Lanka, setting a new record for the fastest century in T20 Internationals.

Off the Field

Beyond the cricket field, Rohit Sharma is known for his philanthropy and sportsmanship. He actively supports various charitable causes and has been involved in initiatives to promote cricket at the grassroots level.
